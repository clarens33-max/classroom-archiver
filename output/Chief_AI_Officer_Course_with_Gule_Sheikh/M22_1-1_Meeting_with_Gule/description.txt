In this course, you have an amazing opportunity to have a 1-1 meeting with your instructor!

The 1-on-1 meeting with Gule aims to allow for open dialogue and tailored advice concerning your educational experience, career goals, and insights you can glean from the instructor's professional journey.

Here are several ways you can utilize your consultation time:
Share and discuss your specific career goals
Gain advice on your educational experience
Discuss the industry and the instructor's professional journey
Receive tailored professional advice
We advise you to arrive with specific goals for your career in mind and be ready to share them with Gule so that she can effectively assist you. While we suggest focusing on one topic for a more efficient session, this is your time; you are welcome to utilize it however you wish.

Duration: 30 minutes

1-1 Meeting Q&A
Q1: What is the purpose of the 1-on-1 meetings? 
These meetings provide you with an opportunity to engage in open dialogue and receive tailored advice regarding your education, career path, and professional insights directly from Gule.

Q2: How long is each meeting? 
Each 1-on-1 meeting is scheduled for 30 minutes to ensure that all students have fair access.

Q3: Can I schedule more than one meeting? 
Due to time constraints and to ensure fairness to all students, you are allowed to book only one 1-on-1 meeting per course.

Q4: How do I cancel or reschedule my meeting? 
If you need to cancel or reschedule, please contact Jordan as soon as you know you won't be able to make it. Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours before the scheduled meeting time.

Q5: What happens if I don’t show up for my meeting? 
Not showing up for your scheduled meeting without prior notice will be marked as a no-show. In such instances, you will be able to reschedule only once all other students with pre-booked sessions have completed their meetings. Please note that rescheduling is not guaranteed and depends entirely on Gule's remaining availability.

Q6: What should I do to prepare for my meeting? 
To make the most of your time, please arrive with clear career goals in mind. Being prepared to share these with Gule will help her provide the most effective assistance during your session.
